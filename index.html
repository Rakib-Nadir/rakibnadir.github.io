About Me

I'm Rakib Mahmud Nadir, a junior offensive security professional with a strong passion for penetration testing and adversary simulation. I focus on manual enumeration, Active Directory attack chains, web/network exploitation, Python/Bash scripting, and AI/LLM security research to identify and demonstrate real-world security weaknesses.

My approach emphasizes realistic threat modeling — from initial reconnaissance through lateral movement to domain compromise — mirroring tactics used by real-world adversaries. I hold certifications including CRTP, eJPTv2, CRTA, CNSP, CSEDP, and CJWPT, with CRTO currently in progress.

Currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Urban and Regional Planning at Khulna University of Engineering and Technology (KUET) (expected December 2027). Ranked in the top 1% globally on TryHackMe. My cybersecurity journey runs parallel to my academic studies, driven by curiosity and a commitment to ethical hacking.
